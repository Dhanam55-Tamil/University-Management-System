About Our Institution

Government Arts and Science College, Jayankondam

About Our Institution

Government Arts and Science College, Jayankondam was established following an announcement made under Rule 110 in the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly on 20 March 2020 by the then Hon’ble Chief Minister Mr. Edappadi K. Palaniswami to open seven new colleges to support rural students in accessing higher education...

Our Mission

To impart knowledge and skills through higher education, to make students aware of their social responsibilities, develop individual personality to meet global challenges, enhance employability, and achieve communal harmony.

Our Vision

To emerge as a center of excellence in higher education, empowering rural youth with knowledge, skills, and values.
